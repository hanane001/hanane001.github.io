Step 01:Enabling Offline Mode

Right now all the devices are not supporting offline speech input.

1. On your device go to Settings -> Language and Input. Click on icon on Google voice input.

2. Under ALL tab select the language you want to download.

3. Once the language package downloaded, you can see it under INSTALLED tab.

step 02: how to to set up a fingerprint lock (for accessing with default commands).

Make sure your device api level is heigher than 23 (marshmallow).

1. Open Settings.

2. Tap Lock screen and security.

3. Tap Fingerprint Scanner.

4. Cover your finger over the scanner repeatedly.

5. Tap DONE.

.6. Tap Turn On.
